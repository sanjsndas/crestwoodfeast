    Frequently asked questions

    Why does my sourdough taste flat?

    Often due to under-fermentation or weak starter. We recommend a 1:1:1 feeding schedule for 3 days before baking.

    What’s the ideal temperature for proofing?

    Between 24–27°C (75–80°F). Use a proofing box or the oven with the light on.

    Can I substitute whole wheat for bread flour?

    Yes, but increase hydration by 5–8% due to higher bran absorption.
